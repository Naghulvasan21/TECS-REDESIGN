Who We Are

The Electronics Club at SASTRA University (TECS) is a community of electronics enthusiasts dedicated to innovation, learning, and practical application of electronic principles.

We provide a platform for students to explore electronics, participate in workshops, work on projects, and compete in national-level events.

Our members gain hands-on experience with various electronic components, microcontrollers, and circuit design, preparing them for real-world challenges.
